Infinite Jump Wall Clip

Game: Zelda: BotW Platform: Switch Hunter: 0x8F3...2a91
Video Player Area

At a specific wall in Hyrule Castle, simultaneously inputting shield surf and bow aim on a specific frame causes physics to malfunction, allowing infinite vertical ascent.

Confirmed reproducible in Ver 1.2.0. A technique often used in RTA (Speedrun), but carries a risk of softlocking in normal play.
